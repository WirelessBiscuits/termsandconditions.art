Statement

"Terms and Conditions" is an interactive nomadic installation based inside a van that aims to make invisible data somewhat tangible.

It will incorporate a range of mediums including; augmented reality, sound and video, as well as installation. The installation will be designed to be highly immersive and engaging, with a focus on both visual and audio elements.

Visually, the installation will incorporate a range of different mediums and techniques, such as sculpture, video, and lighting. These elements will be used to create a dynamic and constantly changing environment that visitors can explore and interact with. Audio will also play a key role in the installation, with a soundscape that will be designed to complement and enhance the visual elements.
